KeyMG

KeyMG is a powerful and user-friendly platform that enables music enthusiasts to create personalized harmonic playlists based on the key and BPM (beats per minute) of songs.

Upon visiting KeyMG, users are greeted with a sleek and intuitive interface. The discover page features a search bar where users can either enter the name of a song they have in mind or browse through a vast collection of popular tracks.

To generate a harmonic playlist we use algorithms and music theory principles to analyze the song key and BPM, ensuring that each subsequent song selected for the playlist harmonically and rhythmically aligns with the user's preferences.
